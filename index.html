How to make a dog happy:

Is there anything in the world better than a happy, wagging tail?

Treats

Dogs love getting treats! Treats can be used for training, encouraging good behavior, or just showing a little extra love!

Toys

Toys are a great way to play with a dog and keep them active! Balls, ropes, and squeaky toys can promote good playful behavior.

Naps

Dogs are excellent nappers, often snoozing through a large portion of the day. Make sure they have a good bed and a sunny spot to curl up in!
